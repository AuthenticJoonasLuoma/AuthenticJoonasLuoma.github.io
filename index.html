< About Me >

Hello there! My name is Joonas Luoma, 32-year-old former ER Nurse, current Software Dev student. I've had interest in technology since I was a little boy and in year 2021 I figured IT engineering might just be the thing for me. So, I made the first steps towards my dream job, left healthcare industry and started studying software development in Tredu, Tampere.

As a person I consider myself to be precise, quick learner, approachable and overall just a fun guy to be around with. I update my skills continuously and try to keep my know-how in pace of the constantly evolving field of technology by reading a lot of fact-based journalism from magazines and from the internet and of course by doing a little programming in my freetime.

I've had experience in many different fields over the past 15 years, for example construction, transport, ski resort, office and healthcare respectively. In the future I see myself working in software engineering. At the moment front-end feels like it's the most approachable option in the field but I'm also interested in back-end development.

In my free time I like to do all kinds of sports but especially skateboarding in the summer and snowboarding in the winter. I play bass in a band called 'Eetu ja Tuukka Avokämmenellä' and I like to compose my own music from time to time as a hobby. I play a few other instruments too and have a small home studio at disposable.
